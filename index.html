Discovery Mastery Assessment

Scenario-Based Evaluation
1
You're interviewing customers about a productivity app. A user says: "I love the app, it's perfect!" What's your best follow-up question to uncover real insights?
A
"Great! What features do you use most?"
B
"Would you recommend it to others?"
C
"Walk me through the last time you used it. What were you trying to accomplish?"
D
"What would make it even better?"
Why C is correct: Generic praise doesn't give actionable insights. By asking for specific behavior and context, you uncover the actual jobs-to-be-done and can identify gaps between what they say and what they do. This reveals real usage patterns and pain points.
